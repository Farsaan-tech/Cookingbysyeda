Dal Tadka

A comforting and flavorful lentil dish made with spices and tempered with ghee. Perfect with rice or roti.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash the lentils and cook them in 3 cups of water until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee/oil, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown, then add tomatoes and green chilies.
  4. Add turmeric, salt, and the cooked lentils. Mix well.
  5. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, then garnish with coriander.

Hyderabadi Biryani

A famous fragrant rice dish made with marinated meat and rich spices, layered for an aromatic experience.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken/mutton, marinated
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices: cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and saffron
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh mint and coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes and boil until 70% done.
  2. In a large pot, layer marinated meat, rice, and fried onions.
  3. Add saffron-infused milk and spices, cover, and cook on low heat.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint and coriander before serving.

Shaami Kebab

Deliciously spiced minced meat kebabs that are shallow-fried and served with mint chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced meat (beef/lamb)
  • 1 cup split chickpeas (chana dal)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: cumin, coriander, garam masala, and salt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak chana dal for 2 hours, then cook with minced meat, onions, and spices until tender.
  2. Blend the mixture to a smooth paste, then shape into kebabs.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the kebabs until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Marag

A flavorful Hyderabadi mutton soup made with spices and herbs, perfect for a cozy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and salt
  • 4 cups water
  • Fresh coriander and mint for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and spices, then the mutton pieces.
  3. Brown the meat, add tomatoes, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add water, cover, and simmer until the mutton is tender.
  5. Garnish with coriander and mint before serving.

Dalcha

A comforting and flavorful lentil dish with a hint of meat and spices, perfect to enjoy with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup toor dal (yellow lentils)
  • 1 cup mutton or lamb, cut into pieces (optional)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Wash the toor dal thoroughly and soak it for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add the mutton/lamb pieces (if using) and cook until browned.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  6. Add soaked dal and water. Mix well and bring to a boil.
  7. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer until the dal and meat are cooked through (about 30-40 minutes).
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander before serving.
